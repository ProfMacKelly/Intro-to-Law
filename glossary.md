#### Rule of Law (system) <a href="#rule-of-law-system" id="rule-of-law-system"></a>

A "durable system of laws, institutions, norms, and community commitment that delivers four universal principles: accountability, just law, open government, and accessible and impartial justice" \[[_What is the Rule of Law_](https://worldjusticeproject.org/about-us/overview/what-rule-law) by [World Justice Project](https://worldjusticeproject.org/), n.d.]
